Progressive Caucus

Who Holds This View

Members of the Congressional Progressive Caucus (CPC), representing urban districts and liberal constituencies.

The CPC is the largest Democratic caucus with 100+ members. Key priorities include Medicare for All, Green New Deal, and economic justice.

Key Priorities

  • Medicare for All / Single-payer healthcare
  • Green New Deal / Climate action
  • Wealth tax and raising minimum wage
  • Criminal justice reform
Blue Dog Coalition

Who Holds This View

Moderate Democrats from swing districts who prioritize bipartisanship and fiscal responsibility.

Blue Dogs represent purple/red districts and must appeal to moderate voters.

Key Priorities

  • Fiscal responsibility / Budget discipline
  • Bipartisanship / Working across the aisle
  • Pro-business policies
  • Balanced approach to healthcare
